Learn to Chain Left-Handed

Home Learn to Crochet Other Resources

Step 1:

Find a comfortable postiton for your hook in your left hand.

Step 2:

Find a comfortable postiton for the yarn in your right hand.

Step 3:

Make a slip knot with your yarn.

Step 4:

Put the yarn on the hook.

Step 5:

Yarn over.

Step 6:

Pull a loop through.

Step 7:

Repeat steps 4-6 until you have the desired number of chains.
